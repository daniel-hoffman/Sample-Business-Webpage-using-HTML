Break it 'til you Make it is a safe environment that serves as a sanctuary for extreme stress relief through the art of smashing random objects. Studies have shown that having built up anger and stress can often lead to unhealthy destructive behavior, so we decided to provide the best source of relief: breaking things.

Our only location as of right now in New York City. Happy hour is 2-4 on the weekdays, but prices triple after a Yankees' loss.
